About AGTU

American Global Tech University (AGTU) is a pioneering institution located in Orlando, Florida, dedicated to providing world-class online education accessible to students worldwide. We partner with prestigious universities — including Harvard, MIT, Cambridge, and UC Berkeley — to offer affordable Associate, Bachelor's, and Master's degree programs. Our PPP-based pricing model ensures quality education is within reach for every student on the globe.
